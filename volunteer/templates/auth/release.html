The Food Pantry at Central Release

This Release by {{ g.user['given_name']}} {{ g.user['family_name']}}, who is referred to below as VOLUNTEER, is on behalf of Central Presbyterian Church of Mobile ("CHURCH"), explained further and referred to below as CHURCH. The Release relates to VOLUNTEER's work for CHURCH's Food Pantry program.

CHURCH in this Release means the Church as an entity and also all people connected with leadership and decision-making for the entity.

VOLUNTEER understands that in working in the Food Pantry program VOLUNTEER is in no sense an employee of CHURCH. VOLUNTEER also understands that the work for the Food Pantry involves risks of physical injury, as it sometime requires volunteers to work in heat or foul weather, to work in auto traffic, to lift heavy packages, to lift boxes from a metal conveyer system, to move heavy loads up and down ramps, to pass through narrow hallways sometimes crowded with people and items on floors , and to otherwise encounter trip hazards that arise from the Food Pantry activity or from the conditions of the Church building, and to do other tasks that involve a risk of physical harm, personal injury (including serious injuries that might lead to death), and damage to or loss of personal property.

VOLUNTEER SPECIFICALLY ACKOWLEDGES THAT THIS RELEASE IS DIRECTED TO CLAIMS AND INJURIES THAT HAVE NOT YET HAPPENED OR ARISEN.

COVENANT NOT TO SUE
